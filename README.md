# villagerimJunior

A small top-down defense adventure made with PocketEngine.

## Current Prototype

- Stage 1: slimes attack the altar. Clear the wave and pick up the bow.
- Stage 2: spear goblins switch targets between the player and altar. Clear the wave and pick up the shield.
- Victory scene: the village survives tonight; chapter 3 comes later.

## Controls

- Move: `WASD` or arrow keys
- Attack: left mouse or `Space`
- Weapon swap: `1` sword, `2` bow after unlocked
- Shield: hold right mouse after unlocked
- Restart from victory: `R` or `Enter`
